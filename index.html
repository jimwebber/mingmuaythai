Meet the Team

Meet our coaches.

  • Coach Ming Luk


    Coach Ming brings over 30 years of Muay Thai teaching experience, including two years of intensive training in Thailand. With authentic Muay Thai techniques and deep expertise, he embodies the true spirit of the art.
    He has participated in numerous international major competitions and has successfully trained multiple fighters in both Hong Kong and the UK. Under his guidance, several fighters have achieved outstanding results, including earning prestigious gold belts.
    His wealth of experience ensures every student receives world-class instruction, whether you’re starting out or preparing for competition.
    He holds a Level 3 Diploma Practitioner in Personal Training (RQF) certificate and is Hyrox certified.

  • Coach Mannie Luk


    Mannie Luk brings over 15 years of rich experience in yoga practice and teaching. With a deep understanding of body structure and movement functions, Mannie specializes in stretching and functional stretching, helping students improve flexibility, strengthen core stability, and enhance mobility and comfort in daily activities.
    Stretching is the foundation of all sports. It effectively improves physical condition and supports students in making continuous progress in Muay Thai and other sports, helping them achieve a better physique and a more fulfilling life.
    Whether you are a beginner or have some experience, Mannie patiently and professionally guides you on a journey of body awareness and holistic improvement. Mannie holds a Level 3 Diploma Practitioner in Personal Training (RQF) certificate.
