Ming Muay Thai

Ming Luk is a seasoned Muay Thai fighter and trainer all the way from Hong Kong. Now based in Guildford, England Ming coaches all ages and abilities. Ming Muay Thai is a friendly club that helps people to get the best out of their training. Whether that’s skills and fitness or even competition-level, the club will help you to grow in confidence and competence.

Training

Although Muay Thai is a contact sport, only those looking to compete or for those who want to sharpen their skills in real-time engage in closely-supervised sparring. For those who want fitness, flexibility and skills sparring is completely optional.
