Tom Clark Wins Fight at Shock 'n' Awe 38

08.09.2025

Ming Muay Thai fighter Tom Clark defeated Farouk Vahdati in a thrilling K1 bout in Portsmouth on 15th March 2025. Tom maintained his steely composure throughout the three rounds, keeping the fight on his terms. He allowed Farouk to tire himself out, and inflicted damaged in short calculated bursts before moving back to safety. Between rounds Tom looked relaxed and rested before launching back in and putting his mark on the fight. Tom won the fight by unanimous judges decision at the conclusion of three rounds.
